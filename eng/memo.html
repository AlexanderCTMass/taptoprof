Executive summary

TapToProf: AI‑native platform for blue‑collar

Integrated business plan & analytical report. March 2026, Pre‑Seed.

Concept: TapToProf — global AI platform connecting blue‑collar pros, replacing human middlemen with AI agents and toxic commission models (20‑30%) with a transparent SaaS subscription for pros ($29.99/month).

Problem: US home services market is estimated at $543B (2025‑26). Incumbents (Angi, TaskRabbit) sell "junk" leads for $15–120 or charge double‑sided commissions up to 30% (BBB, 2024). Pros waste 80% time on messaging, clients drown in spam.

Solution: Zero‑UI (photo+voice) → AI orchestrator → transparent auction + pro subscription. Conservative pro LTV $720, CAC ~$180, LTV/CAC = 4. At 10,000 pros ARR reaches $3.6M (subscription only).

legacy is broken

Problem: US market pain points

Client side

High entry barrier: to call a plumber you need to fill forms, compare 10+ replies. Lack of trust: work photos can be downloaded from Pinterest, verification is a major bottleneck (Juniper Research, 2024).

Pro side

Angi sells one lead to 3‑8 contractors, closed‑deal cost can reach $1400–2500 (BBB complaints, 2025). TaskRabbit takes 15‑30% commission + client fee 5‑9% (TaskRabbit ToS, 2026). A pro on a $100 job nets ~$80.

Structural stagnation

Monopolists benefit from chaos: they monetize every contact. The market is ripe for disruption.

user journeys

User scenarios (USA)

Client Anna (Denver): snaps a photo of a faucet, speaks → AI recognises, launches auction among nearby plumbers. Within a minute gets an offer at $90 with confirmation.

Pro Alexander: opens app, sees requests, sets price or enables "autopilot" (AI bids within $80‑120). At month end pays $29.99 subscription.

multi‑agent orchestration

AI multi‑agent system

Agent orchestrator: Classifier (CV+NLP), Scheduler (calendars, Maps), Negotiator (price prediction, auto‑bidding), Reputation (scrapes Google Maps, Yelp, detects fake reviews), Summary (reviews, reports).

Stack: Python (FastAPI), LangChain, OpenAI/Claude, YOLOv8, PostgreSQL (pgvector), Stripe Connect.
